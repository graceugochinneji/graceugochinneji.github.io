Projects

Under the supervision of Dr. Grace Ugochi Nneji, our research group is dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence through real-world, application-driven projects. Our portfolio spans critical domains such as healthcare, climate change, renewable energy, bioinformatics, and drug discovery. Each project combines deep learning, attention mechanisms, explainable AI (XAI), and domain-specific insights to develop robust, interpretable, and deployable solutions.

From retinal disease diagnosis using multimodal fundus imaging to solar anomaly classification via attention-enhanced convolutional networks, our students tackle high-impact challenges by fusing theory with cutting-edge engineering.
